## 1. Getting Started

### Sign Up

1. Open [https://math-homework-management.web.app](https://math-homework-management.web.app)
2. Click **Get started** or **Sign up**
3. Choose **Continue with Google** for instant sign-in, or enter your email and password
4. On the **Onboarding** screen, select your role:
   - **Teacher** — you manage classes and assignments
   - **Student** — you receive and submit assignments
5. Click **Continue** — you are taken to your dashboard immediately

### Log In (returning users)

1. Visit the app and click **Sign in**
2. Use the same method you signed up with (Google or email/password)
3. You are redirected to your role-appropriate dashboard automatically

### Forgot Password

1. On the login screen, click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter your email — a reset link is sent within 1 minute
3. Click the link in the email and set a new password

## 2. Teacher Guide

### 2.1 Creating Classes and Batches

**First class:**
When you sign in for the first time, the dashboard prompts you to create your first class.

1. Enter a **Class Name** (e.g., "Year 7 Maths")
2. Optionally set **Class Type** (Solo / Group), **Day**, and **Time**
3. Click **Create Class**

**Additional classes:**
1. On the Dashboard, look for the **+ New Batch** button in the batch pills row at the top
2. Fill in the class details and click **Create**
3. Switch between classes by clicking the class name pills

---

### 2.2 Managing Students

**Add a student:**
1. Navigate to **Students** in the top navigation
2. Click **Add Student**
3. Enter the student's email address
4. Click **Add** — the student receives a welcome notification immediately

**Remove a student:**
1. Find the student in the list
2. Click the **Remove** (trash icon) button next to their name
3. Confirm the removal — they lose access to this class's assignments

**Move a student between classes:**
1. Remove them from the current class (see above)
2. Add them to the new class using their email

> **Note:** Students can belong to multiple classes simultaneously.

---

### 2.3 Creating AI Worksheets

1. Go to **Worksheets** in the top nav
2. Click **Generate New**
3. Fill in the worksheet generator form:
   - **Grade Level** — e.g., Grade 7
   - **Topic** — e.g., "Quadratic equations"
   - **Example Problem** — paste a sample question to guide the AI style
   - **Difficulty** — Easy / Medium / Hard
   - **Number of Problems** — 5 to 20
   - **Due Date** — pick from the date picker
4. Click **Preview** — the AI generates a preview worksheet in ~5 seconds
5. Review the problems. Click **Regenerate** if you want a different version
6. Click **Assign to Class** — individual PDFs are generated and delivered to every student
7. A confirmation modal appears: _"Successfully assigned the worksheet"_

> **Tip:** Generated worksheets are stored. You can re-assign them later without regenerating.

---

### 2.4 Creating File Assignments

1. On the **Dashboard**, click **+ New Assignment**
2. Select the **File Upload** tab
3. Enter an **Assignment Title** and optional **Description**
4. Drag and drop (or click to browse) your PDF or image file
5. Set a **Due Date**
6. Click **Assign** — all students in the selected batch receive a notification

---

### 2.5 Monitoring the Dashboard

The dashboard shows a card for every active batch assignment:

| Element | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| Progress bar | Visual ratio of submitted / total students |
| `12 / 25 Submitted` | Exact count, updates in real time |
| `13 Pending` | Students who have not yet submitted |
| `View Submissions` button | Opens the full submission review table |

> **Real-time:** When a student submits, the counter increments automatically — no refresh needed.

---

### 2.6 Reviewing and Grading Submissions

1. Click **View Submissions** on any assignment card
2. The submission table shows every student with:
   - Upload link (click to view their file)
   - Submission date and time
   - AI-suggested grade (percentage, auto-calculated)
   - Status pill: Submitted / Needs Grading / Graded
3. To grade a student:
   - Edit the score field (pre-filled with AI suggestion)
   - Add optional feedback in the comments box
   - Click **Confirm** — the student immediately sees their grade
4. Use the **filter pills** (All / Submitted / Needs Grading / Graded) to narrow the list
5. Use the **search box** to find a specific student
6. Click **Export CSV** to download all results for reporting

---

### 2.7 Tracking Student Progress

1. Click **Progress** in the top navigation
2. Select a **Class** from the dropdown
3. Select a **Student** from the second dropdown
4. The page shows:
   - **5 stat cards**: Total Assignments, Submitted, Missing, Late, Average Score
   - **Bar chart**: Score per assignment over time, with class average reference line
   - **Assignment history table**: Every assignment with status, grade, AI grade, and comments
5. Use the **status filter pills** to view only missing or late work
6. Use the **search box** to find a specific assignment

---

### 2.8 Using the Calendar

1. Click **Calendar** in the top navigation
2. Toggle between **Month** and **Week** view using the buttons at the top right
3. **Add a session:**
   - Click on any day (month view) or time slot (week view)
   - Fill in the session details: class, time, type, notes
   - Click **Save** — the session appears immediately
4. **View session details:**
   - Click any session chip to open the detail popup
   - Shows time, class, type, and notes
5. Use the arrow buttons to navigate between weeks/months
6. Click **Today** to jump back to the current date

## 3. Student Guide

### 3.1 Viewing Assignments

1. After logging in you land on **My Assignments**
2. Each card shows:
   - Assignment title
   - Due date (with colour: green = plenty of time, amber = due soon, red = overdue)
   - Status badge: **Not Submitted** / **Submitted** / **Graded**
3. Click any card to open the submission panel

---

### 3.2 Submitting Work

1. Click the assignment card you want to submit
2. In the submission panel, drag and drop your file — or click **Browse** to select
   - Accepted formats: **PDF, JPG, PNG, JPEG, TXT**
   - Maximum file size: 10 MB
3. Click **Submit**
4. The card status changes to **Submitted** immediately
5. Your teacher's dashboard updates in real time

> **Note:** You can only submit once. Contact your teacher if you need to resubmit.

---

### 3.3 Viewing Grades and Feedback

1. Once your teacher confirms your grade, the assignment card changes to **Graded**
2. You receive an in-app notification (bell icon in the navbar)
3. Click the card to see:
   - Your score (percentage)
   - Teacher feedback / comments

---

### 3.4 Earning Badges

Badges are awarded automatically when you hit milestones:

| Badge | How to Earn |
|-------|------------|
| First Step | Submit your first assignment |
| Gold Star | Score 90% or above |
| Silver Star | Score 75%–89% |
| High Achiever | Average score above 85% across 5+ assignments |
| Streak (3-day) | Submit 3 days in a row |
| Streak (7-day) | Submit 7 days in a row |
| Monthly Champion | Top scorer in your class for the month |
| Math Master | Complete 20+ assignments |
| Perfect Score | Score 100% on any assignment |

View your badges any time on the **Badges** page from the top nav.

## 4. Troubleshooting & FAQ

### Q: I signed up but I'm stuck on a blank screen
**A:** Make sure you completed the onboarding step and selected your role (Teacher or Student). Try signing out and back in.

### Q: I added a student by email but they can't see the class
**A:** The student must sign up using the exact email address you entered. Once they log in and select the **Student** role, the assignments will appear.

### Q: The AI worksheet generation is taking a long time
**A:** Generation can take up to 10 seconds. If it times out, try reducing the number of problems or refreshing and retrying. Check your internet connection.

### Q: A student submitted but the counter didn't update
**A:** Real-time updates require an active internet connection on both ends. Refresh the page — the count will be correct after a reload.

### Q: I confirmed a grade by mistake
**A:** Grades can be re-edited from the Submission Review table. Find the student, change the score, and click Confirm again.

### Q: The PDF link isn't opening
**A:** Some browsers block pop-ups. Allow pop-ups for this site, or right-click the link and choose "Open in new tab".

### Q: I can't upload my file — it says "unsupported format"
**A:** Only PDF, JPG, PNG, JPEG, and TXT files are accepted. Convert your file to one of these formats and try again.

### Q: I lost my password and the reset email hasn't arrived
**A:** Check your spam/junk folder. If using Google login, use "Continue with Google" instead — no password is needed.

### Q: Can a student be in more than one class?
**A:** Yes. Teachers can add the same student email to multiple classes. The student will see assignments from all classes in their portal.
